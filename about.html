Get Knitted With Us .

So, this website is created by three young aspiring minds- us-who always wanted to do something, apart from grilling through our graduation degree and finally we are here at the KnittingThoughts. Here our purpose is to make people realize how great they are by delivering some daily experiences we face which one might fail to realize and thus become distant from the very first person we were supposed to know - our own self. Above all else, it would be quite a fun to relate the same experience that I had today with which you had yesterday or will be having tomorrow – kind Of déjà vu.

Who We Are.

We are three friends currently in our sophomore, who share some common aspects of life and a motive to rediscover the lost faith in sharing tastes and experiences in this roof of passing times..

Our Mission.

We happen to experience different types of stuff every day, with shades of good and bad. But do we care to acknowledge them? Sometimes several societal facts surround us with difficulties to think whether it would be right to share some particular thoughts or experiences. Probably because we don't realize these things could be worth sharing until it's told by someone else. Only then we get to know how fine and awesome we are to keep up with so much hardships daily, enduring all sorts of pain and sorrow and still moving on and dreaming the next day.

Our Vision.

In this Virtual world of living elements, like us, who thrive to share their experiences and to pen their thoughts, we three came up with this virtual place in order to put an end to this long wait with our creation - KnittingThoughts. Life’s a path and a journey worth sharing. So, in this generation of seemingly unavoidable work-loads and day-to-day managements, let us get knotted in the threads of thought, knitted by us all where you can share and relate your unending real life experiences with others.

Our Values.

By creating this place for minds to gather, we just happened to ensure the thoughts that arise and the feelings that cater to us are not unique in all ways. All our experiences are books in the book shelves with wonderful stories to tell. So, why wait? Start sharing and keep moving forward.
